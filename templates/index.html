Nuclear explosions

this website contians data about all folowing nuclear test up until the year 2000. This website uses information from

this data set.

Our data shows that the biggest nuclear bomb (as far as TNT and shockwave goes) is the TSAR bomba detonated by the soviet union over the Novaya Zelya island with an explosion of about 50 megatons of TNT (50,000,000 pounds of TNT), for refrence the bomb dropped on Nagasaki that killed 60,000-74,000 people had an explosion of about 21 kilatons of TNT (26,000 pounds of TNT OR 0.021 megatons).

So within this website you will be able to find information such as bombs dropped/tested per year, amount of bombs dropped/tested per country, along with a graph of the increase in nuclear bomb drops/test per year. This comes fromthe data set which includes inoformation such as, the name, the region it was dropped, the cordinates it was dropped, the type of test, the date, the source of the info, the yeild of the bomb in kilatons (the high and low estimte), the depth it was detonated from the ground, the reason the test was conducted, and the method in which they dropped/used the bomb
